Welcome to Ge'ez Biotech

At Ge’ez Biotech, we are reimagining how Ethiopia sources essential industrial and agricultural inputs. Our mission is simple: produce high-quality enzymes and microbial bioinputs, locally. With growing demand and rising import costs, we believe Ethiopia is ready for its own sustainable biotech revolution—one that is rooted in science, self-reliance, and purpose.

  • Modern fermentation platforms (submerged and solid-state)
  • Custom-formulated culture media using local agro-waste

Our Story

Ge’ez Biotech was founded on a belief that biotechnology can—and should—grow from within Africa. What started as a side project in a garage-like lab has grown into Ethiopia’s first independent enzyme and biofertilizer venture. We chose the name Ge’ez to reflect deep cultural roots and a vision for growth grounded in knowledge and resilience.

We are scientists, farmers, engineers, and dreamers. Every product we release is not just a formula—it’s a small piece of a larger mission: to give Ethiopia control over what it grows, builds, and exports. Our journey is just beginning, and we invite you to be part of it.

Vision & Mission

  • Vision: To make Ethiopia a hub for affordable, eco-friendly industrial biotechnology.
  • Mission: Reduce dependency on imports by creating local alternatives for enzymes, biofertilizers, and biopesticides.
  • Support small and large businesses with tailored biotech inputs.
  • Employ and train Ethiopian scientists, technicians, and entrepreneurs.
